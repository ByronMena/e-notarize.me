Identification Requirement

To perform a notarization, each signer must present valid, unexpired government-issued identification that includes a photograph and signature. Acceptable forms of identification align with Florida Statute § 117.05(2)(a-j)

Non-Attorney Disclosure

BM Notary Services LLC is not a law firm, and our notaries are not attorneys licensed to practice law. We cannot provide legal advice, representation, or recommendations. If you have questions about your documents or require legal advice, please consult with a qualified attorney.

Document Preparation Responsibility

Clients are solely responsible for selecting and preparing their documents before notarization. BM Notary Services LLC does not advise on document selection, drafting, or interpretation.

Limited Notary Role

Our notaries serve solely as impartial witnesses to the signing of documents and verification of signers' identities. We do not verify the truthfulness, accuracy, or legality of document contents.

Deposits are applied toward the total cost of the service.

Deposits are refundable only if cancellation is made with at least 24 hours' advance notice.

1.2 Payment Methods

Payments can be made via credit/debit card or other methods specified during the booking process.

  • $25 RON Deposit (Square)
  • $100 for loan signing appointments (Square)

Appointments are confirmed only when:

An appointment is not finalized without a confirmation email.
